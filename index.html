About Us

REA is a development company, that offers a high quality real estate in Italy, the Switzerland and the South Africa. With proven competencies in properties and hospitality, REA creates and shapes a family lifestyle in less populated areas with more lake views, nature, and quiet atmosphere with a focus on high-end design, innovative technologies, and timely delivery.

Environment policy

REA is committed to protecting the environment and ensuring the sustainability of our communities. We are aware of the importance of preserving natural resources and work to implement practices that are environmentally sustainable. Our goal is to create harmony between economic development and environmental sustainability.
