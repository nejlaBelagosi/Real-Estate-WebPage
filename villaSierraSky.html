Infrastructure

Lake Garda is the largest lake in Italy.

It is a popular holiday location in northern Italy, about halfway between Brescia and Verona, and between Venice and Milan on the edge of the Dolomites. Glaciers formed this alpine region at the end of the last ice age. The lake and its shoreline are divided between the provinces of Brescia (to the south-west), Verona (south-east) and Trentino (north).

Costermano sul Garda is a comune in the Province of Verona in the Italian region Veneto, located about 120 kilometres west of Venice and about 25 kilometres northwest of Verona.

They say that good things come in small packages, and this intriguing city is no exception to the rule. From popular tourist attractions to exciting excursions, there are loads of fun things to do in Costermano.
